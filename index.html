Our Service Concept: An Extended Description

Using Chegg— which provides digital and physical textbook rentals, online tutoring, and other student services, such as textbook/expert solutions— as the basis for the idea, we formulated the concept of a note-sharing platform, through which students would be able to offer notes taken based on subjects/topics, textbooks, and video lectures. Supplementary materials, such as videos or articles that students found helpful in explaining a given topic, would be available through the note-sharing service as well. In addition, there were a few features that we figured would contribute to making our application more reliable from a student’s perspective. Chegg utilizes the availability of certified experts on a variety of topics in order to ensure the quality of textbook solutions/answers to students’ questions. We wanted to implement a similar system through which we would be able to approve notes submissions or students’ additions to previously submitted notes before they’re put up, as well as a feedback system in order to gauge how helpful the notes were. Overall, the service would make notes far more accessible to students who physically may not be able to take notes, or simply prove to be convenient/helpful to students trying to understand/solidify topics while developing more efficient note taking strategies.
